Hello! Welcome to Origami Designs

Our goal is to show more people how to make origami while helping them improve their skills and promoting artists and others
in the international paper folding community.

If you’ve never folded anything before and want to learn how, we have an excellent guide for beginners. We have a giant database
of free diagrams and video instrutions too. Our blog features amazing work from a variety of very talented artists, we also have a collection of
book reviews and recommendations plus much more!

Panda


Interesting Facts about Panda


Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.

Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.

An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
